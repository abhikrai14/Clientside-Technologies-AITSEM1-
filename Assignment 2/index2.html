AIT Computer Society

The AIT Computer Society is open to all who have an interest in learning new computer skills. Meetings have a friendly atmosphere where members come to showcase their latest inventions, or maybe get some advice on how to start!


Each week focuses on a particular topic from a variety of areas including Web Design, Hardware Prototyping and Machine Learning.
